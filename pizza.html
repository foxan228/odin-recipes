Pizza "Pepperoni"

Description

Pepperoni is a variety of spicy salami made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika or other chili pepper

Prior to cooking, pepperoni is characteristically soft, slightly smoky, and bright red.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Smoked sausage
  • Chilli
  • Tomatoes in their own juice
  • Oregano
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. First, make the sauce: mix tomatoes with spices, minced garlic, sugar, salt and pepper.
  2. Bring to a boil and cool.
  3. Roll out the dough, brush it with olive oil and sauce.
  4. Sprinkle with half the cheese, lay out the sausage and thinly sliced chili.
  5. Top with the remaining cheese.
  6. Cook in the oven for 10-12 minutes at 220 degrees.
